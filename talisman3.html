Take the Talisman to the Pawn Shop

The Wooded Hustle

Lily decides that the talisman might be worht some money, and decides to take this mysterious and ancient talisman to the pawn shop and make a few dollars.

She makes her way to the village. She found a small pawn shop on the outskirts of town and entered, curious about what the owner might offer for the ancient object.

The Pawn Shop

The pawnbroker examined the talisman carefully and offered her a modest sum of money for it. Lily accepted, grateful for the come up. Lily had no idea she was derailing her destiny, but her self-centered motives would prove to her there was a need for change.

As the days passed, Lily knew she had made a mistake, but was unable to find the crossroads where her journey started. She had gone off the rails as it pertained to her destiny.
