Take a Seat and Reflect on the Moment with the Butterfly

Reflection and Pondering

Lily sits on an old mossy stump for a short while thinking about her attempts to catch the butterfly. Her mind starts racing with thoughts of the capture.

Something about this butterfly has changed Lily from the inside out, and she is beginning to believe she may be under a spell.

The tree woman speaks to Lily and explains that the magical butterfly has sprinkled enchanted dust on her nose. She has 30 seconds before she turns into the same.

Lily turns into a tree and is suspended in time. She can never leave the forest.

The End
